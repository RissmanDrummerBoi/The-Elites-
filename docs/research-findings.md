## Student Findings

### How issues are reported
- **RESPONSES**
- I write a text to a residence representative and go to residence offices
- We have to directly go to the maintenance and report for ourselves
- I have to go to maintenance building
- Take a walk to the maintenance office
- To the res warden
- You go physically to the maintainance site
- I go to the mantainance
- In person or via groups
- You go and report your problem, at maintenance they will come with many excuses regarding your problem and they will not help you in time.
- Go direct to maintenance office

## Key Issues Identified
- No centralized reporting system
- Poor communication between users and staff
- Lack of tracking and feedback

## Initial System Requirements
Based on current findings:

- Online issue reporting form
- Clear categories (Plumbing, Electrical, etc.)
- Status tracking (Pending, In Progress, Resolved)
- Notification system
